About me:

I am a small town gamer who has always had a dream of creating his own game. I wish to one day create a game that would have the same impact on the indie game industry as some of the bigger titles, like Undertale and Hollow Knight. Something that would invoke great amounts of emotion in people.

An image of the cover art for Blasphemous, a game that inspired me to try and make my own games.

A list of Goals:

  • I wish to publish at least 5 small games and 1-2 big games with DLC.
  • I wish to create at least one board game/card game in my life.
  • I wish to get better at my artwork and fill out 5 big sketch books.
  • After I've gotten some experience from writing my games, I wish to write a book.
